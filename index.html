About WAS

A message from the Founder

The World Archive System (WAS) is a project built to preserve humanity's collective knowledge using cutting-edge decentralized technology.

WAS is designed with a micro-services architecture spanning multiple specialized domains — from developer tools (was.tools), to operational infrastructure (was.network), to public access endpoints (was.ngo).

At WAS, we believe that information is our most precious resource. By preserving and connecting knowledge from our past, we create the foundation for a brighter future.

WAS—named for the was-scepter, the sacred instrument guide's humanity toward knowledge and truth, connecting our historic wisdom with tomorrow's innovations.
